THE FRIENDLY RPG WHERE NOBODY HAS TO DIE
NOTICE: Pressing the green flag will NOT:
　> Delete your save
　> Reset your settings
Reload the page if you want to delete your progress.

　　　--- Instruction [Controller is supported] ---
　　　[Z] - Confirm [A, R]
　　　[X] - Cancel [B, L]
　　　[C] - Menu (In-game) [X, Y, R2, L2]
　　　[F11] - Fullscreen [Null]
　　　[Hold ESC] - Quit (Scratch ✖) [Null]
　　　When HP is 0, you lose.

　　　--- Additional Controls ---
　　　[1] - Music toggle [Null]
　　　[2] - Sound toggle [Null]
